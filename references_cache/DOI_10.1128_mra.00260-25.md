# Using DNA affinity purification sequencing (DAP-seq) to identify
            <i>in vitro</i>
            binding sites of potential
            <i>Novosphingobium aromaticivorans</i>
            DSM12444 transcription factors
**Authors:** Kevin S. Myers, Alexandra M. Linz, Leo A. Baumgart, Yu Zhang, Ian Blaby, Ritesh Mewalal, Vivian Ng, Daniel R. Noguera, Timothy J. Donohue
**Journal:** Microbiology Resource Announcements (2025)
**DOI:** [10.1128/mra.00260-25](https://doi.org/10.1128/mra.00260-25)

## Content

ABSTRACT



              Genome-wide binding sites of 44 putative transcription factors (TFs) from
              Novosphingobium aromaticivorans
              DSM12444 were analyzed using DNA affinity purification sequencing. We report that 32 of these TFs have at least one area of enrichment. These data will help better understand aromatic metabolism and other features of
              N. aromaticivorans
              biology.
