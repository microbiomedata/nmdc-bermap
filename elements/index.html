    NMDC SFAs and BRCs Schema

    A LinkML schema for representing DOE BER (Biological and Environmental Research) funded Scientific Focus Areas (SFAs) and Bioenergy Research Centers (BRCs), including their metadata, datasets, publications, and organizational structure.

    URI: https://w3id.org/nmdc/sfas-brcs

    Name: nmdc-sfas-brcs
